Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about nano coating and our services

How long does nano ceramic coating last?

+

Our professional-grade ceramic coatings last 2-7 years depending on the product and maintenance. We offer different packages based on your protection needs and budget.

Is ceramic coating better than wax?

+

Yes, ceramic coating bonds chemically with your paint and lasts years, while wax only provides surface protection for a few months. Coating also offers superior protection against UV rays, chemicals, and scratches.
